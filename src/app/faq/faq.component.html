Frequently asked questions.

Here you can read frequently asked questions, if you haven't found an answer to something, write to us at support

Advantage of being Admin

Admin can create quizzes, share it, manage it.

 Advantage of being user

User can take quizzes and view their results.

Why guest?

As guest you can take quizzes, but you can't see your results, you need to add email in your profile.

 How to create quiz?

You can create quiz only when you register as admin.

How quiz works?

Every good answer gives you 10 points, wrong 0 points, either you choose from the answer you have to choose from or you write the answer in to the answer field.
